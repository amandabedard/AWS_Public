Creya: Journey to the Otherworld

Estimated Release:

This game has an estimated release date of late 2019

About the Game:


Discover a vast new world as you help Moosie and Bleu uncover the mystery of
why everyone in town has suddenly gone missing.


Explore intricate dungeons crawling with enemies and challenges.


Meet quirky characters all across the map as you slowly work to uncover the
truth behind this mystery.
